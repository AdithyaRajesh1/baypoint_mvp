# Multi-Agent Pipeline Suggestions for Alternative Asset Deals

## Core Analysis Agents

### 1. **Legal & Structure Analysis Agent**
- Analyzes legal structure, entity types, and corporate governance
- Reviews regulatory compliance (SEC, FINRA, etc.)
- Examines partnership agreements, operating agreements, and fund documents
- Identifies legal risks and structural concerns
- Assesses jurisdiction and regulatory environment

### 2. **Financial Modeling & Valuation Agent**
- Performs DCF analysis, comparable company analysis, and precedent transactions
- Models cash flows, returns (IRR, MOIC), and exit scenarios
- Analyzes fee structures (management fees, carried interest, performance fees)
- Evaluates capital structure and leverage
- Assesses valuation methodologies and assumptions

### 3. **Risk Assessment Agent**
- Identifies market, credit, operational, and liquidity risks
- Analyzes concentration risk (geographic, sector, counterparty)
- Evaluates downside scenarios and stress testing
- Assesses correlation with existing portfolio
- Reviews risk management frameworks and controls

### 4. **Market & Sector Analysis Agent**
- Analyzes market trends, growth drivers, and competitive landscape
- Evaluates sector-specific dynamics and cycles
- Assesses market size, penetration, and opportunity
- Reviews competitive positioning and differentiation
- Analyzes macroeconomic factors and market timing

### 5. **Due Diligence Agent**
- Reviews operational capabilities and track record
- Analyzes management team quality and alignment
- Evaluates business model sustainability
- Assesses technology, systems, and infrastructure
- Reviews historical performance and consistency

### 6. **Tax & Structuring Agent**
- Analyzes tax implications (income, capital gains, estate tax)
- Evaluates tax-efficient structures (LLCs, LPs, trusts)
- Reviews pass-through vs. corporate tax treatment
- Assesses international tax considerations
- Identifies tax optimization opportunities

### 7. **Liquidity & Exit Strategy Agent**
- Evaluates liquidity profile and lock-up periods
- Analyzes exit strategies (IPO, sale, secondary market)
- Assesses secondary market availability
- Reviews redemption terms and restrictions
- Evaluates time horizon and liquidity needs alignment

### 8. **Compliance & Regulatory Agent**
- Reviews SEC registration requirements (if applicable)
- Analyzes accredited investor requirements
- Evaluates anti-money laundering (AML) compliance
- Assesses KYC requirements and procedures
- Reviews regulatory reporting obligations

## Sector-Specific Agents

### 9. **Real Estate Analysis Agent** (for real estate deals)
- Analyzes property fundamentals (location, quality, occupancy)
- Evaluates cap rates, NOI, and cash-on-cash returns
- Assesses property management and operations
- Reviews lease terms and tenant quality
- Analyzes market fundamentals and supply/demand

### 10. **Private Equity Analysis Agent** (for PE deals)
- Evaluates buyout vs. growth equity strategies
- Analyzes operational improvement opportunities
- Assesses add-on acquisition potential
- Reviews portfolio company management
- Evaluates value creation levers

### 11. **Venture Capital Analysis Agent** (for VC deals)
- Analyzes startup stage and funding round
- Evaluates product-market fit and traction
- Assesses team quality and market opportunity
- Reviews competitive landscape and defensibility
- Analyzes exit potential and timeline

### 12. **Hedge Fund Analysis Agent** (for hedge fund investments)
- Evaluates investment strategy and edge
- Analyzes risk-adjusted returns (Sharpe, Sortino ratios)
- Assesses correlation with market and other strategies
- Reviews portfolio construction and risk management
- Evaluates manager track record and consistency

### 13. **Infrastructure Analysis Agent** (for infrastructure deals)
- Analyzes regulatory environment and concessions
- Evaluates long-term cash flow stability
- Assesses inflation protection mechanisms
- Reviews operational requirements and maintenance
- Analyzes public-private partnership structures

### 14. **Commodities & Natural Resources Agent**
- Analyzes supply and demand fundamentals
- Evaluates extraction/production costs
- Assesses environmental and regulatory risks
- Reviews commodity price cycles and volatility
- Analyzes storage and transportation logistics

### 15. **Private Credit Analysis Agent** (for private debt)
- Evaluates credit quality and default risk
- Analyzes loan terms, covenants, and security
- Assesses recovery rates and collateral
- Reviews interest rate risk and duration
- Evaluates credit enhancement structures

## Specialized Analysis Agents

### 16. **ESG & Impact Analysis Agent**
- Evaluates environmental, social, and governance factors
- Assesses impact metrics and alignment
- Reviews sustainability practices
- Analyzes ESG risk exposure
- Evaluates impact measurement frameworks

### 17. **Technology & Innovation Agent**
- Analyzes technology stack and scalability
- Evaluates intellectual property and competitive moats
- Assesses digital transformation potential
- Reviews cybersecurity and data privacy
- Analyzes innovation and R&D capabilities

### 18. **Geographic & Political Risk Agent**
- Evaluates country and regional risks
- Analyzes political stability and regulatory environment
- Assesses currency and exchange rate risks
- Reviews geopolitical considerations
- Evaluates cross-border transaction complexities

### 19. **Performance Benchmarking Agent**
- Compares to similar alternative asset deals
- Analyzes peer performance and metrics
- Evaluates relative value and pricing
- Reviews industry standards and best practices
- Assesses performance attribution

### 20. **Portfolio Fit & Allocation Agent**
- Evaluates correlation with existing portfolio
- Analyzes diversification benefits
- Assesses portfolio allocation and sizing
- Reviews concentration limits and constraints
- Evaluates strategic fit and objectives alignment

## Implementation Priority

### Phase 1 (Essential):
1. Legal & Structure Analysis Agent
2. Financial Modeling & Valuation Agent
3. Risk Assessment Agent
4. Market & Sector Analysis Agent
5. Due Diligence Agent

### Phase 2 (Important):
6. Tax & Structuring Agent
7. Liquidity & Exit Strategy Agent
8. Compliance & Regulatory Agent
9. Portfolio Fit & Allocation Agent
10. Performance Benchmarking Agent

### Phase 3 (Sector-Specific):
11-15. Sector-specific agents based on deal types you handle most

### Phase 4 (Advanced):
16-20. Specialized analysis agents for comprehensive coverage

## Integration Suggestions

- **Sequential Pipeline**: Run core agents first, then sector-specific, then specialized
- **Parallel Processing**: Run independent agents simultaneously for speed
- **Conditional Routing**: Route to sector-specific agents based on deal type
- **Synthesis Agent**: Final agent that combines all analyses into comprehensive report
- **Confidence Scoring**: Each agent provides confidence scores for their analysis
